Raj R. Jhaveri, CPA

Raj started the practice in 1984, and has been serving clients ever since. He holds the practice to a high standard, especially as it concerns the firm's traditional values of integrity, personalized attention and excellence in accounting. His prior experience includes positions as Internal Auditor, Controller, Director of Finance, and VP Finance and Operations at various companies. His accreditations include BS Accounting, MA Accounting, Law graduate (ranking #1 of more than 1000 students), Chartered Accountant, Cost and Works Accountant, and CPA.

Roma Jhaveri, CPA

Roma specializes in tax and tax planning. She is especially excited about helping small businesses and individuals succeed in their financial lives. She spent a decade working as a software engineer, product manager and entrepreneur before joining her father's practice. As such, she brings a more technology-oriented perspective to the practice. Roma holds a BS in Computer Science and a MS in Management Science & Engineering from Stanford University, and is a CPA.
